This is a WebGL project designed to simulate the fictional environment of planetary system 4546B from the game Subnautica (as closely as possible).

Change the camera view to see what the simulation looks like from different locations, or use the mouse to move the camera while in the 4546B System view.

One day on 4546B lasts 20 Earth minutes. Use the Time Multiplier slider to speed this up.

Note to grader: The sun is located behind the camera, you will have to turn the camera around to see it.

In addition, some light sources are easier to see in the scenes other than default. (Or are too weak to be seen on a sunlit surface)
